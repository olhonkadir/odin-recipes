Hot Cheesy Fries

Hot cheesy fries for cheesy lovers

Ingredients

  • frozen fries
  • cheddar cheese
  • Monterey jack cheese
  • pickled jalapenos
  • sour cream

Method

  1. Bake the fries according to the instructions on the packaging.

  2. Remove the baked fries from the oven and top them with shredded cheese, sliced jalapenos, and chopped bacon.

  3. Return the loaded fries to the oven for an additional 5-10 minutes or until the cheese has fully melted.

  4. Remove the fries from the oven, drizzle sour cream over them, and serve them while still warm.
